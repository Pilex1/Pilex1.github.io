About

North Bay is a newly developed central district to the north of the original two CBDs of Dominion City. The district contains a vibrant mixture of commercial, hotel, and residential skyscrapers. In addition, the central park of North Bay is surrounded by a complex housing the General Assembly of the Ezrean Dominion, making it a politically important region. Central Station is located on the southern fringe of North Bay, and interchanges the Tri-City, Western, Far South, and Northern Beaches Lines of the Dominion Rail Network. North Bay is most well known for its avant garde modern architecture, and is home to Pacific Tower, the tallest building in the Ezrean Dominion.
