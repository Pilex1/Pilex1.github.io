About

Sponsorton is a regional town in the Dominion. It is located east of Dominion City and is serviced by the Far East Line. Sponsorton was founded in 2018, with the construction of the Post Office and the town centre.

The Sponsorton Light Rail provides public transport access to Sponsorton Railway Station from the surrounding streets of Sponsorton. The main North-South line connects Sponsorton Railway Station with Main Street, including the Post Office and Town Hall. The main East-West line connects the Railway Station with the surrounding residential streets, as well as the Sponsorton Sign.
