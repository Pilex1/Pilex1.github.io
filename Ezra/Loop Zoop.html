About

Loop Zoop is a suburb of Dominion City, in the Ezrean Dominion. It is situated south of Capital Plateau in a region comprising marshes and plains and comprises residential developments of both houses and high-rise apartments, as well as an industrial area.

Loop Zoop was named for the loops of the highway interchange that dominates the area, joining the Great South Highway with the H1 Intercity. It is served by the Loop Zoop/Far South Railway Line.
