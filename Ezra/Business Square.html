About

Business Square was the initial centre of the Western CBD, founded after development of Harbourside, the original CBD of Dominion City, reached capacity. It is now home to some of the tallest skyscrapers of Dominon City and is a densely populated zone of residential, commercial, and hotel uses. The square which the suburb is named after is surrounded by headquarters for many large companies including TACO Engineering, Citadel Construction, ONEGroup, and TSU.
