About

Los Corvino West and Los Corvino South skyline

Los Corvino West is an urban centre and suburb of Dominion City. It is a centre for entertainment, hotels, and casinos for the Greater Dominion City Area. Its urban core consists of many hotel and resort developments, as well as high end residential towers.

Los Corvino West was originally part of the expansion of the semi-autonomous suburb of Los Corvino. It separated from Los Corvino in 2016 and became a distinct entity in order to secure tax and regulation exemptions for casino and hotel construction. Los Corvino West is served by the High Line, which terminates at Los Corvino South Station, as well as the proposed Los Corvino Metro which will connect Los Corvino West and Los Corvino.
